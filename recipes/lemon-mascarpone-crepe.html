Lemon Mascarpone Crepes

Description

A thin buttery pastry filled with mascarpone infused with a tart, zesty flavor.

Ingredients

Crepes

  • 375ml whole milk
  • 300ml all-purpose flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 30ml unsalted butter
  • 15ml granulated sugar
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • 5ml vanilla extract

Whipped lemon mascarpone filling

  • 250ml mascarpone cheese
  • 45ml granulated sugar
  • 125ml whipping cream
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 2ml pure vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Add flour, milk, eggs, butter, sugar, salt and vanilla to a blender and blend on high for 2 minutes. Rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Add mascarpone cheese and sugar to a stand mixer with the whisk attachment and whip on medium speed. Slowly add the cream and whip for 2 to 3 minutes. Add the lemon zest, juice and vanilla, whip until fluffy. Keep filling in the fridge.
  3. Heat a 10 inch frying pan over medium high and add enough butter to coat the bottom of the pan.
  4. Ladle about 75ml of batter and swirl the pain in a circular motion. Cook for about 1 minute until the crepe is golden and the top looks set. Flip and cook for another minute. Place cooked crepe on a plate. Sprinkle granulated sugar to prevent crepes from sticking together.
  5. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  6. Spread the desired amount of filling on each crepe, roll them up, dust with icing sugar.
  7. Top with ice cream and maple syrup if desired. Serve immediately.

Recipe taken from Penguin Random House Canada.
