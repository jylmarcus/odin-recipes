Coffee Biscuits

Description

A buttery biscuit with a subtle coffee flavor.

Ingredients

  • Butter, 227g
  • Whole roasted coffee beans, 50g
  • Sugar, 200g
  • Eggs, 2
  • Vanilla extract, 5ml
  • All-purpose flour, 525g
  • Cornstarch, 100g
  • Kosher salt, 6g

Steps

  1. Put the butter in a small pot set over low heat and let it melt completely.
  2. Add the coffee beans and let them soak in the butter for 1 hour over the lowest heat setting. More time will yield a stronger coffee flavour.
  3. Strain the coffee beans from the butter and discard them.
  4. Put the coffee butter in the refrigerator to cool and solidify.Put the coffee butter in the refrigerator to cool and solidify.
  5. In a large bowl, cream together the sugar and the coffee butter until they are light and fluffy.
  6. Add the eggs and vanilla and mix until they are fully combined.
  7. Add the flour, cornstarch and salt and mix until a crumbly dough forms.
  8. Separate the dough into 2 to 4 balls, depending on the size of your work surface.
  9. Place a ball of dough between two pieces of parchment paper and roll it out to ¼” or 6.5 mm thick.
  10. Use a cookie cutter to cut out shapes and transfer them to a parchment paper lined baking sheet.
  11. Chill the cut dough in the freezer for 10 minutes.
  12. Preheat the oven to 325°F/163°C.
  13. Bake the cookies for 6 minutes, rotate and switch the pan positions, then bake for another 6 minutes.
  14. Let the cookies cool completely before decorating.
